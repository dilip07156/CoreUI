ACCOMMODATION TAX:

Kindly note accommodation tax have been levied by local governments in the below respective cities. These taxes cannot be collect at our end and hence TL has to pay the hotel directly at the time of check out.

Florence / Padova / Rome / Venice / Barcelona/ Lisbon/ Paris

Local Municipality will apply an accommodation tax to all their guests staying, FIT or groups.

The payment of the tax is mandatory and to be paid directly by the clients at the time of check out. 01 TL per group is exempted from the accommodation tax.
